About Us

Established in 1985, The Rock Junior High School has a proud tradition of academic excellence and holistic development. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive education that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a strong sense of community. We believe in creating a supportive environment where every student can thrive and reach their full potential. We are located in Darkuman, Accra, Ghana.

Our dedicated faculty are experts in their fields, passionate about teaching, and committed to guiding students on their educational journey. We offer a balanced curriculum that combines rigorous academics with a wide range of extracurricular activities, ensuring students are well-prepared for college and beyond.

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to meet national standards while challenging students to think critically and creatively across all subjects.
